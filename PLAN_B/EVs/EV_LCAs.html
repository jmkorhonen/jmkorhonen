Nandhakumar 2020, ELECTRIC BUS FRAME

  • Examines the effects of changing the chassis frame of Sunwin SWB6121EV57 electric bus from A709M grade 345 W structural steel to aluminium 6061-T6 or 7075-T6.
    • Gross vehicle weight + payload 18 000 kg
    • 60 passengers
    • gross weight of passengers 65 kg = 3900 kg
    • total load 21 900 kg
  • Weight original 11 504 kg
    • 6061-T6 3957.9 kg (65.61 % reduction)
    • 7075-T6 4103.2 kg (64.33 % reduction)
  • 7075-T6 shows less stress and deformation, making it an optimum material.
